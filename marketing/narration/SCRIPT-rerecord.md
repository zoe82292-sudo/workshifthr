**[intro]**  
If you’ve ever spent days in Excel before merit week — or anytime leadership asks who’s underpaid — you know the fear: you might still miss something. I built ShiftWorks HR for that moment. Upload your compensation census and in under thirty seconds you get who needs attention, what it’ll cost to fix, and a summary leadership can actually use.

**[upload]**  
You already have the file — Workday, ADP, UKG, or a spreadsheet. Drop it in. No new HRIS. Nothing for IT to configure. You’re ready.

**[dashboard]**  
First you get a prioritized list of what to fix before letters go out — not fifty tabs to dig through. Critical items rise to the top so you know where to start.

**[budget]**  
Then you get the number finance will ask for: what it costs to bring people up to range, what’s in the merit pool, and your total exposure. So you’re not guessing in the meeting.

**[issues]**  
Every flag is a real person — name, department, and the exact dollar gap. You can act on it the same day, instead of rebuilding another spreadsheet.

**[managers]**  
It also catches the awkward ones — like a manager earning less than someone they lead. You see both sides clearly, so you can handle it before it becomes a surprise.

**[equity]**  
When leadership asks about pay fairness, you can show median pay by gender and race, and the gap between groups — from your own data, ready for the conversation.

**[location]**  
If location is in your file, you see how pay looks by city and where your people sit — without building a separate geo analysis.

**[pdf]**  
Need to brief executives? One click — risk level, total exposure, and the talking points they’ll ask about.

**[cta]**  
Try ShiftWorks HR free with your own census. No credit card required.
