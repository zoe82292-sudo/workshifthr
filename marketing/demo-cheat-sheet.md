# Call cheat sheet — ShiftWorksHR

**Show:** https://shiftworkshr.com/sample-preview

---

## Product (30 sec)

Compensation analysis on a census export (Workday / UKG / ADP / Excel).  
Severity-ranked queue, $ gaps, inversions, budget, pay equity signals, leadership PDF — under a minute.  
**Not only merit** — also range reviews, mid-year, promotions, post-hiring waves, anytime leadership asks who’s out of range.  
**Not** ATS, payroll, HRIS, or legal pay-equity audit.

---

## Personal struggle (60–90 sec)

People say “just do it in Excel.” You can — I did.  
Doing it *well* = **days** (messy file / interruptions → toward a **week**). Easy to miss a flag.  
Built ShiftWorksHR so the export becomes a prioritized queue + PDF in under a minute.  
**Not replacing Excel or Workday — replacing the archaeology.**

---

## Why buy vs configure a platform

Platforms = system of record (good, slow, expensive to configure).  
ShiftWorksHR = answers **this week** on the file you already have.  
> “Upload, don’t configure.”

---

## Who it’s for

**Mid-size companies (~150–2,000 employees)**  
Comp / total rewards · HRBPs / People ops · consultants serving them  

> Big enough for real ranges; small enough to still live in Excel — not waiting on an Advanced Comp project.
